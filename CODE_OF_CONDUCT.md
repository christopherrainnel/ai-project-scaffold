# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and inclusive community for all contributors, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We expect respectful collaboration across issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and technically specific.
- Assume good intent, but focus on observable behavior and outcomes.
- Give clear feedback on code and ideas, not personal attacks.
- Acknowledge mistakes and apply corrections quickly.
- Protect privacy and confidentiality.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, hate speech, or intimidation.
- Sexualized language or unwanted sexual attention.
- Trolling, insulting, or demeaning personal comments.
- Doxxing or publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warning.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-managed spaces, including repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and communication channels used for project collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcement and may take proportionate action, including:

1. Informal warning and guidance.
2. Formal warning with required behavior changes.
3. Temporary suspension from community spaces.
4. Permanent removal from project participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to maintainers. Do not post sensitive reports publicly.

- Primary route: direct private contact with repository maintainers.
- Security-related concerns: follow `SECURITY.md`.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
